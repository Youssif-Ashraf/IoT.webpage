Introduction

The Internet of Things (IoT) is modern communication, in which the objects (like all household appliance ) of everyday life will use microcontrollers for digital communication. The IoT concept aims to make the Internet even more pervasive. Moreover, by allowing easy access and interaction with a lot of variety of devices such as home appliances, surveillance cameras, so on.
