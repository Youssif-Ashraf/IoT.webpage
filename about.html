internet of Things

The Internet of things is a way of interrelated computing devices, and digital machines provided with the ability to transport data over a network without needing human-to-computer interaction. The meaning of the Internet of things has grown due to the convergence of many technologies, real-time analytics, machine learning, and embedded systems. Internet of things has many uses in our life we will mention some of them. [2]

smarter catastrophes management

With enough intelligence to predict with accuracy, the onset of conditions that increase forest fires before they get out of handle or even begin, allow rescue teams to respond faster while enabling first responders to manage targeted evacuations better and more aligned to the real situation in the field. This concept may also fit with the flood detection or, earthquakes and other natural disasters.

smarter healthcare

Devices that discover a gathering of health problems, potentially before they even happen like heart attack, and quickly administer life-saving drugs or deploy emergency responders with detailed information placed instantly in their hands or alert family members as-needed. [3]
