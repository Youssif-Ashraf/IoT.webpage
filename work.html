how internet of things work

The Internet of Things also announced as the Internet of Everything (IoE), defined as all the web-enabled devices that collect, and send data they round up from their around environments using embedded sensors, processors and hardware. These devices often called "smart" devices, can communication with other relevant devices, a process called machine-to-machine (M2M) communication, and manage the information they get from one another. Humans can communicate with the gadgets to set them up, give them instructions or the data, but the devices do most of the work on their own without human intrusion. [4]
